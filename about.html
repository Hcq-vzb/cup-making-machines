About Us

Our Factory

Jiangsu KIWL Machinery Manufacturing Group Co., Ltd is located in Zhangjiagang City, Jiangsu Province, China. We specialize in manufacturing high-speed paper cup machines, full servo paper cup machines, paper bowl machines, salad bowl machines, double-wall machines, coffee cup machines. We have more than 10 years of experience in this domain. Our company has strong technical strength and excellent equipment, and has the capacity to independently develop new products. Meanwhile, it has passed the ISO9001-2000 quality management system and the EU CE. Our machines are exported to numerous countries, such as Turkey, India, Dubai, Russia, Switzerland, Portugal, and so on. We provide high quality at a reasonable price. If you have an interest in our machines, please feel free to get in touch with us.

Our Service

After the customer confirms the order, we will send you a drawing in accordance with the requirement of your cup size. After the customer confirms the drawing and we have received the deposit, we will commence to produce the machine. During the process, we will update the machine production schedule to the customer. Before we deliver the machine, we will send you the machine video. Once everything is ready, we will deliver the machine. When the machine arrives at the customer's factory, we will dispatch the technical personnel to assist in installing the machine.
