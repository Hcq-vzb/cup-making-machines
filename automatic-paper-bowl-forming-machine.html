Product Description

The XSL-1350T Automatic Paper Bowl Forming Machine utilizes cam transmission and spray lubrication for its components. This greatly improves the operational efficiency and durability of the machine. Precisely controlled by PLC and accurately monitored by photoelectric sensors, it guarantees stable and reliable performance. This Automatic Paper Bowl Forming Machine is extremely suitable for producing 16-46 OZ paper bowls commonly used for ice cream and noodles, catering to the diverse demands of the market. Its advanced technology and efficient production capacity make it the preferred option for many manufacturers in the industry.

Technical Parameters

Paper Cup Sizes : 16OZ-46OZ
Cup Top Diameter: 100-150mm
Cup Bottom Diameter: 80-125mm
Cup height: 50-135mm
Cup Speed : 100-120 pcs/min
Machine Net Weight: 4500kg
Rated Power: 24kw
Air Consumption: 0.6-0.8Mpa
Machine Size: L3000*W1500*H2060MM
Paper Grams: 150-380sm(double Pe)PE/PLA paper

Advantage

The advanced Automatic Paper Bowl Forming Machine possesses several remarkable advantages. It enhances production efficiency by rapidly manufacturing a considerable number of paper bowls to satisfy market requirements. It features energy-saving characteristics and stable performance, minimizing downtime and guaranteeing efficient production.It ensures the quality and uniformity of the paper bowls through precise control. The advanced automation system lessens manual labor, reduces costs and mistakes.
